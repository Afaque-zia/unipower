Your information is safe and secure and we do not publish any of your information without your approval. CONTROL OF YOUR PASSWORD?

We protect your credit card information during transmission by using the Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) protocol, which encrypts your information when transmitted over the Internet. None of this information is saved on USP coin servers.

COOKIES?

Cookies are small pieces of information that are stored in a browser-related file on YOUR computer’s hard drive when you use our Web site. They are used so the site can recognize your computer and provide you with personalized information. We use cookies to improve the user experience. Some features of our website require the use of cookies. For example, if you create a shortlist of favourite cottages, we need to be able to recognize your computer on your next visit. To do this we send your computer a unique code (as a cookie) that we can read. When you want to see your list of favourite cottages, we read that code and use it to find your favourites’ Cookies are widely used on most major Web sites.
